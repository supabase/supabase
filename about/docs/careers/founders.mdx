## About the role

The Supabase team is a group of founders who previously scaled businesses to millions in revenue, hundreds of employees, and worked in a multitude of industries:
fintech, healthcare & medtech, crypto, insurance, agritech, marketplaces, and of course - open source dev tools.

We recognize the value of the founder mindset.
If you're a founder and you're looking for an opportunity to work with other founders, tell us how you can help Supabase succeed and we'll give you the resources to make it happen.
