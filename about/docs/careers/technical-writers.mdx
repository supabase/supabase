## About the role

Do you know what amazing documentation looks like?

Do you have experience building for the web?

Are you an engaging writer with an eye for detail?

Responsibilities includes:

- Blog posts for the Supabase website
- Helping other team members communicate their wins with edits and suggestions
- Writing internal docs such as: documenting processes, how-to guides etc.
- Writing and Organizing our documentation, and generally setting the direction on how they are structured
- Helping with enterprise orientated marketing collateral: thought leadership, white papers, case studies
- Working directly with the Founders, Growth, and Dev Rel teams on strategy

Must Have:

- Intermediate understanding of Postgres
- Full stack development experience
- Fluent in English
- Track record of producing content for a developer audience
- Enjoys working online remotely with minimal supervision

Nice to Have:

- Understanding of SEO
- Previous experience with SaaS content marketing
