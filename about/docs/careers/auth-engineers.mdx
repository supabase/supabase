## About the role

- [Auth](https://supabase.com/auth) is one of the most popular products in the Supabase stack
- Be a Maintainer of our Open Source Auth repos written in [golang](https://github.com/supabase/gotrue) and [typescript](https://github.com/supabase/gotrue-js)
- Be a key decision maker in the Supabase Auth team
- Expand our auth offering and work with our users to prioritize the product roadmap
- Previous working experience of Authorization and Authentication is a must
- Experience working with OAuth, SSO, SAML etc.
- Advise our users on how best to make use of Gotrue and Postgres RLS policies
- Help grow the Auth team
