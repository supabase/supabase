## About the role

- Play a key role in establishing the marketing and growth strategies for supabase
- Working cross-functionally with our engineering and growth teams to ensure we have the best possible messaging and marketing strategy
- Communicating the results of our marketing strategy internally
- Liaise and work with our incredible DevRel team to help them identify opportunities and amplify their efforts
- Work with our amazing team of community writers to help find topics to write about
- Understand what's driving our user acquisition funnel
- Keep our social media feeds active
- Create and manage email campaigns
- Helping with community management

## Skills and Experience 

- Experience with marketing automation and CRM tools
- Experience with analytics platforms
- SEO experience
- Creating and managing social media campaigns
- Marketing to software developers & developer tooling space
- Understanding of product led growth & developer communities
- Metrics driven & experimental mindset

## Nice to haves

- Familiarity with dbt / Segment / Hightouch / Snowflake / Redshift or other Modern data tools is a plus
- Experience with Youtube optimization
