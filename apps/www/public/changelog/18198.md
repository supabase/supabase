# Postgres 12 Deprecation Notice

- number: 18198
- published: 2023-10-14
- discussion: https://github.com/orgs/supabase/discussions/18198
- labels: postgres
- page: https://supabase.com/changelog/18198

---

Postgres 12 is deprecated as of 14th October 2023 and support for it will be fully removed on 27th November 2023. 

Postgres 15 comes with numerous features, bug fixes and performance improvements. Check out the announcement blog posts to find out what each version introduces.
- [Postgres 13 Launch](https://supabase.com/blog/supabase-postgres-13)
- [Postgres 14 Launch](https://supabase.com/blog/whats-new-in-postgres-14)
- [Postgres 15 Launch](https://supabase.com/blog/new-in-postgres-15)

## Deprecation Timeline
- **15th October**: All users are notified via email about Postgres 12 Deprecation.
- **27th October**: Users can self serve upgrade to Postgres 15 [from our dashboard](https://supabase.com/dashboard/project/_/settings/infrastructure). If you want to upgrade your database to Postgres 15 before 27th October, [reach out to our support](https://supabase.com/dashboard/support/new). A dashboard notification will be sent about this deprecation. 
- **13th November**: Users are notified via email. 
- **27th November**: All Postgres 12 databases are automatically upgraded to Postgres 15. 

You will receive three notifications via email before 27th November notifying you about the deprecation of Postgres 12 and [deprecation of IPv4 and PGBouncer](https://github.com/orgs/supabase/discussions/17817).
