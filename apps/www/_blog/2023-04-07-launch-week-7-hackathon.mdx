[Launch Week](https://supabase.com/launch-week) is about to start, and we're running a hackathon alongside it, and this time we are doing an AI-themed hackathon!

The official Supabase Hackathon starts now and ends Monday 16th April at 11:59 pm.

You can win $1500 in GitHub sponsorships and official limited edition Supabase swags!

For inspiration, check out all the [winners from previous hackathons](https://supabase.com/blog/tags/hackathon).

## Key Facts

- You have 10 days to build a new **Open Source** project using Supabase in some capacity.
    - Starting 8:00 am PT Friday 7th April 2023
    - The submission deadline is 11:59 pm Sunday midnight PT 16th April 2023
- Enter as an individual, or as a team of up to 5 people
- Build whatever you want - a project, app, tool, library, anything
- There are 5 prize categories.
    - Best overall project will win Supabase keyboards, and a limited edition Supa AI Hackathon swag kit.
    - Will all win a limited edition Supa AI Hackathon swag kit for each team member!
    - Extra credits for all the AI projects!

## Prizes

There are 5 chances to win, there will be prizes for:

- Best overall project (Supabase Keyboards & Swag kit)
- Most fun/best easter egg (Swag kit)
- Most technically impressive (Swag kit)
- Most visually pleasing (Swag kit)
- Best AI project (Swag kit)

There will be a winner and a runner-up prize for each category. Every team member on winning/runner-up teams gets a Supa AI Hackathon swag kit.

## Submission

You should submit your project using [this form](https://www.madewithsupabase.com/launch-week-7) before 11:59 pm Sunday midnight PT 16th April 2023. Extra points if you include a simple video demoing the app in the description.

## Rules

- Team size 1-5 (all team members on winning teams will receive a prize)
- You cannot be on multiple teams
- One submission per team
- All design elements, code, etc. for your project must be created **during** the event
- All entries must be Open Source (link to source code required in entry)
- Must use Supabase in some capacity
- Extra credit to use AI in some capacity
- Can be any language or framework
- You must submit before the deadline (no late entries)
- Include a link to a demo video along with the submission for extra points
