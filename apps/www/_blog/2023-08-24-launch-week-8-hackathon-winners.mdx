[Launch Week 8](https://supabase.com/launch-week) passed by, but we still have our favorite part left, celebrating the winners of the hackathon! We've had some exceptional results and the quality of these apps are out of this world. You can view all the submissions on [madewithsupabase.com](https://www.madewithsupabase.com/).

And now, without further ado, congratulations to the winners of Supabase Launch Week 8 Hackathon!

## Best Overall Project

### Winner

[WITAS - Wait is that a sticker?](https://github.com/alex-streza/witas) - by [@alex_streza](https://twitter.com/alex_streza) and [@Catalina_Melnic](https://twitter.com/Catalina_Melnic)

WITAS, it's an acronym for Wait is that sticker, in a nutshell it generates stickers with Midjourney.

![WITAS - Wait is that a sticker?](/images/blog/lw7-hackathon-winners/01_page_assist.png)

### Runner Up

[Stumble - Agree with someone](https://github.com/devkrafts/Stumble-more-like-bumble) - by [@ayushjn\_](https://twitter.com/ayushjn_) and [@anushka\_\_shukla](https://twitter.com/anushka__shukla)

Stumble let's anyone decide and agree upon something be it a place to eat, a place to hangout or some activity. Any 2 people can create a link, join a room, start swiping, the Bumble way until they swipe the same place or activity. This helps in deciding things a lot faster.

![Stumble - Agree with someone](/images/blog/lw7-hackathon-winners/02_rect.png)

## Best use of AI

### Winner

[Tweeets](https://github.com/Steellgold/tweeets) - by [@Steellgold](https://twitter.com/Steellgold)

Generate tweets using AI, based with your own tweets or your own parameters. Share your tweets models with your friends and the world.

![Tweeets](/images/blog/lw7-hackathon-winners/03supathreads.png)

### Runner Up

[AI COLOR: colorize photo with AI](https://github.com/EyuCoder/aicolor) - by [@eyucoder](https://twitter.com/eyucoder)

If you're looking to colorize old black and white photos, this AI photo colorizer app can help you bring your memories back to life.

![AI COLOR: colorize photo with AI](/images/blog/lw7-hackathon-winners/04_chatvox.png)

## Most fun / best easter egg

### Winner

[Supafactor: Does your README have it?](https://github.com/danryland/supafactor) - by [@RealDanRyland](https://twitter.com/realdanryland)

Taking a page from Rick and Morty's vibrant visual style and combining it with the thrill of talent shows, I introduce to you: Supafactor - a groundbreaking way to judge hackathon entries.

![Supafactor: Does your README have it?](/images/blog/lw7-hackathon-winners/05_replayai.png)

### Runner Up

[BeerBet](https://github.com/ChouquetteCorp/beerbet) - by [@BilelJegham](https://twitter.com/BilelJegham) and [@PolThm](https://github.com/PolThm)

Experience a whole new way to enjoy your favorite matches with our sports betting app centered around fun and beers, without any money wagered.

![BeerBet](/images/blog/lw7-hackathon-winners/06_grooveai.png)

## Most technically impressive

### Winner

[Openbin](https://github.com/ethndotsh/openbin) - by [@ethndotsh](https://github.com/ethndotsh) and [@jack\_\_merrill](https://twitter.com/jack__merrill)

Openbin is a Pastebin clone built for command line warriors, with support for both a speedy and accessible Monaco web editor, as well as an efficient Go CLI.

![Openbin](/images/blog/lw7-hackathon-winners/07supa0sql.png)

### Runner Up

[Supalytic](https://github.com/Ansh-Rathod/schedurio) - by [@suciptodev](https://twitter.com/suciptodev) and [@andreecy](https://github.com/andreecy)

Supalytic is website analytic tracking alternative to Google Analytics. It built with svelte kit, supabase auth, database and deployed on cloudflare pages.

![Supalytic](/images/blog/lw7-hackathon-winners/08_schedurio.png)

## Most visually pleasing

### Winner

[Doors of Abyss: The Endless Chase](https://github.com/laznic/doors-of-abyss/) - by [@laznic](https://twitter.com/laznic)

Doors of Abyss: The Endless Chase is an interactive browser game. It has an original story which went through the AI to improve the language used (because it was an amateur hour when we wrote it ourselves). Images have been generated via AI (Midjourney and Stable Diffusion).

![Doors of Abyss: The Endless Chase](/images/blog/lw7-hackathon-winners/09_generation_hotdog.png)

### Runner Up

[Supadraw - Draw like an artist](https://github.com/arbizen/supadraw) - by [@arbizzen](https://twitter.com/arbizzen)

Supadraw - Draw like an artist is a simple game where it plays a random song in reverse and you have to guess the name of the song.

![Supadraw - Draw like an artist](/images/blog/lw7-hackathon-winners/10_twisters.png)
