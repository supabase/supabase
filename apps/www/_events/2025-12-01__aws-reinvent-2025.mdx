### Why Supabase?

Supabase is the open-source Firebase alternative that gives you:

- **100% Postgres Database**: No forks, just pure Postgres with all its power and extensions
- **Built-in Authentication**: Secure auth with social logins, magic links, and Row Level Security
- **Realtime Subscriptions**: Build collaborative apps with live data synchronization
- **Edge Functions**: Deploy serverless functions globally with Deno
- **Vector Embeddings**: Native pgvector support for AI/ML applications
- **Storage**: S3-compatible object storage for files, images, and videos
