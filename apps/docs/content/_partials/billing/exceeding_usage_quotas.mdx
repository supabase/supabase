If you are on a paid plan and have [Spend Cap](/docs/guides/platform/cost-control#spend-cap) disabled or your organization is on a >=Team Plan, you will simply pay for any overages.

When you are exceeding your quotas while being on a Free Plan or having [Spend Cap](/docs/guides/platform/cost-control#spend-cap) enabled, you will be get a notification to your billing email address and put on a grace period.
If you either greatly exceed the quota again or exceed the quota in your next billing cycle, your organization and projects will be restricted. For more details, refer to our [Fair Use Policy](http://localhost:3001/docs/guides/platform/billing-faq#fair-use-policy).
