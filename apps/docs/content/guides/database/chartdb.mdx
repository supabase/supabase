[`ChartDB`](https://chartdb.io/) is an open source database diagramming tool. You can use it to visualize your Supabase database schema with live ERD diagrams that automatically sync.

<Admonition type="note">
  ChartDB only reads your database schema metadata. It does not access your actual data.
</Admonition>

<StepHikeCompact>

  <StepHikeCompact.Step step={1}>

    <StepHikeCompact.Details title="Create New Diagram">

    Go to the [ChartDB app](https://app.chartdb.io/) and click **New Diagram** to open the diagram creation dialog.

    </StepHikeCompact.Details>

  </StepHikeCompact.Step>

  <StepHikeCompact.Step step={2}>

    <StepHikeCompact.Details title="Select Supabase Database">

    In the database selection screen, choose **Supabase** (the green lightning bolt icon), then click **Continue**.

    </StepHikeCompact.Details>

    <StepHikeCompact.Code>

     ![Select Supabase database](/docs/img/guides/database/connecting-to-postgres/chartdb/select-supabase-database.png)

    </StepHikeCompact.Code>

  </StepHikeCompact.Step>

  <StepHikeCompact.Step step={3}>

    <StepHikeCompact.Details title="Choose Cloud Platforms Connection">

    On the import method screen, select **Cloud Platforms** which shows the OAuth option.

    </StepHikeCompact.Details>

    <StepHikeCompact.Code>

     ![Connect with Supabase OAuth](/docs/img/guides/database/connecting-to-postgres/chartdb/connect-with-supabase-oauth.png)

    </StepHikeCompact.Code>

  </StepHikeCompact.Step>

  <StepHikeCompact.Step step={4}>

    <StepHikeCompact.Details title="Authenticate with Supabase">

    Click the **Connect with Supabase** button. A popup window will open directing you to the Supabase authentication page. Log in with your Supabase account credentials and authorize ChartDB to access your projects.

    <Admonition type="note">
    Make sure popups are enabled for ChartDB in your browser.
    </Admonition>

    </StepHikeCompact.Details>

  </StepHikeCompact.Step>

  <StepHikeCompact.Step step={5}>

    <StepHikeCompact.Details title="Select Project and Enter Password">

    After successful authentication, you'll see a list of your Supabase projects. Select the project you want to visualize, then enter your Supabase database password.

    You can find your database password in your [Database Settings](/dashboard/project/_/database/settings).

    </StepHikeCompact.Details>

    <StepHikeCompact.Code>

     ![Select project and enter password](/docs/img/guides/database/connecting-to-postgres/chartdb/supabase-project-password.png)

    </StepHikeCompact.Code>

  </StepHikeCompact.Step>

  <StepHikeCompact.Step step={6}>

    <StepHikeCompact.Details title="Connect & Import">

    Click **Connect & Import** to start the synchronization process. ChartDB will import your schema metadata and redirect you to your new diagram.

    </StepHikeCompact.Details>

  </StepHikeCompact.Step>

  <StepHikeCompact.Step step={7}>

    <StepHikeCompact.Details title="Re-sync Your Database">

    Your Supabase database automatically syncs every hour. To manually trigger a re-sync, click the **Connected** button in the top navigation bar and select **Re-sync Now**.

    </StepHikeCompact.Details>

    <StepHikeCompact.Code>

     ![Re-sync database](/docs/img/guides/database/connecting-to-postgres/chartdb/supabase-connection-status-resync.png)

    </StepHikeCompact.Code>

  </StepHikeCompact.Step>

</StepHikeCompact>
